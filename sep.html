Platforms such as GameDay and MyTimes help teams and athletes maintain player statistics and published race results over many seasons.

Historical records — including APS boys swim and dive results — document performance across generations of competition. Alongside sport, local community development in regions such as the Sunshine Coast continues to shape the environments where athletes train and families live.
